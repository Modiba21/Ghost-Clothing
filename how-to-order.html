How to Order

Ordering from Ghost Clothing is simple. Follow the steps below to complete your purchase quickly and securely.

Step 1: Browse Products

Go to our Shop page and browse through available clothing items such as T-shirts, hoodies, and accessories.

Step 2: Select Your Item

Click on the product you want, choose your size and quantity, then add it to your cart.

Step 3: Go to Cart

Review your selected items in the cart. Make sure sizes, quantities, and details are correct.

Step 4: Checkout

Proceed to checkout where you will be redirected to secure payment via PayFast or other supported payment methods.

Step 5: Make Payment

Complete your payment securely using your preferred method (card, EFT, or mobile payment options depending on PayFast availability).

Step 6: Order Confirmation

Once payment is successful, you will receive an order confirmation and we will begin processing your order immediately.

Step 7: Delivery

Your order will be shipped and delivered within the estimated timeframe provided at checkout. You will be updated throughout the process.
