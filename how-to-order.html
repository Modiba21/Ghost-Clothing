How to Order

Ordering from Ghost Clothing is simple. Follow the steps below to complete your purchase quickly and securely.

Step 1: Browse Products

Go to our Shop page and browse available clothing items.

Step 2: Select Your Item

Choose size and quantity, then add to cart.

Step 3: Go to Cart

Review your items before checkout.

Step 4: Checkout

Proceed to secure payment via PayFast.

Step 5: Payment

Complete payment using available methods.

Step 6: Confirmation

You will receive order confirmation after payment.

Step 7: Delivery

Your order will be delivered within estimated timeframe.
