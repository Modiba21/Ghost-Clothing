Returns & Refund Policy

At Ghost Clothing, we value customer satisfaction. If you're not happy with your purchase, you may request a return or exchange under the conditions below.

1. Overview

At GhostClothing, we want you to be satisfied with your purchase. If you are not completely happy, we offer refunds or exchanges under the conditions outlined in this policy.

2. Returns Eligibility

Items must be returned within 7 days of delivery, unused and in original condition.

3. Non-Refundable Items

Worn items, damaged after delivery items, and sale items (unless faulty).

4. Exchanges

Allowed depending on stock availability. Customer covers return shipping unless faulty.

5. Refund Process

Refunds are processed after inspection and returned to original payment method.

6. Refund Timeframes

5–10 business days depending on bank/payment provider.

7. Damaged Items

Report within 48 hours with proof for replacement or refund.

8. Shipping Costs

Non-refundable unless item is faulty.
